Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

1931 – 2015 | The Missile Man of India 🚀

Biography

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a pivotal role in India's missile development programs and nuclear tests, earning him the title "Missile Man of India."

Major Achievements

  • Key figure in India’s Pokhran-II nuclear tests (1998).
  • Developed ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology.
  • Served as the 11th President of India (2002–2007).
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna, India’s highest civilian award.

Famous Quotes

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
"If you want to shine like a sun, first burn like a sun."

Legacy

Dr. Kalam inspired millions of students across India. His dedication to education, innovation, and national development continues to motivate young minds to dream big and work hard.
